Making tabletop games in Wichita, KS? Yes.

Our concept for Wichita Startup Weekend Spring 2014, a card game called "Bang & Burn", won the Judge's Choice grand prize. We won some great prizes (including a 12 month lease on the 14th floor of a Downtown Wichita office building!)
But, we didn't win any money. So we are bootstrapping and getting the game design together to launch a Kickstarter to fund production of the game and the mobile app. We've decided to rename the game "Crash & Burn" to better identify as a game and not a dating app... It doesn't hurt that the name doubles as a reference to the influential movie Hackers.

If you check back here or sign up for our newsletter we will keep you informed about game progress and will let you know when the Kickstarter launches!
